"Pet Heart Fit: Your Trusted Partner in Raising Happy, Well-Trained Dogs for a Balanced and Joyful Life."

Pet Heart Fit is a leading dog training service provider in India, dedicated to helping pet parents raise well-mannered, confident, and happy dogs. With a strong focus on compassionate and positive training methods, Pet Heart Fit has earned the trust of pet owners across the country. The brand believes that every dog deserves a chance to thrive, and every owner deserves support in building a strong, respectful bond with their furry companion.

The company offers a diverse range of training programs tailored to dogs of different ages, breeds, and behavioral needs. These include Puppy Training, Basic Training, Intermediate Training, Smart Training, Advanced Training, and the All-in-One Training package, which combines key elements from all levels for a complete development experience. For dogs facing behavioral challenges such as aggression, anxiety, or disobedience, Pet Heart Fit also provides specialized Canine Behaviour Therapy (CBT), designed to address and correct problematic behaviors in a constructive, humane way.

Understanding that different environments influence learning, Pet Heart Fit extends its services beyond home sessions by offering training at its serene farmhouse location. The farmhouse-based programs—available for Puppy Training, Basic Training, and Intermediate Training—allow dogs to train in a peaceful, distraction-free setting, making the learning process more enjoyable and effective. These sessions are ideal for dogs that benefit from open spaces and hands-on socialization under expert supervision.

Each program at Pet Heart Fit is led by experienced and certified trainers who prioritize your dog’s well-being while delivering measurable results. Their methods rely on positive reinforcement, patience, and consistency, ensuring that your dog learns not only obedience but also confidence and trust. Whether you're welcoming a new puppy or seeking to correct long-standing behavioral issues, Pet Heart Fit is your reliable partner in raising a well-trained and emotionally balanced dog.

Why Choose Us?
